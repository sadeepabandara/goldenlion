About Us.

History.

Every great city deserves a truly great international hotel - a special place that reflects the importance and the beauty of its location with world-class luxury and the very finest service. golden-lions Colombo is delighted to welcome you to the enchanting capital of Sri Lanka a precious jewel in the Indian Ocean, with a glittering cultural heritage and a long, proud history.The hotel benefits from a prime location by the sea that is in the heart of the business district and buzzing social hotspot. A personal tropical sanctuary that is perfect for escaping the city, it offers some of the finest, most modern accommodation in the country, with 350 luxurious guest rooms and suites, and 32 serviced apartments. An exciting new dining and social scene has come to life around the hotel's outstanding group of restaurants and bars, and the exclusive Horizon Club Lounge is the largest of its kind in Colombo.The hotel offers direct access to the most extensive international shopping mall in Sri Lanka, GoldenLion's own Lions Mall caters to the best of modern lifestyle needs from shopping, dining, entertainment, fashion, beauty and technology.

New-to-market concepts that guests can enjoy include GoldenLion's own Adventure Zone, a fun and immersive play space for those aged two to twelve, and Trainyard, an innovative workout hotspot.
