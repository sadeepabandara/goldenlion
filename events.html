Meetings & events

Goldenlions Colombo has the most extensive and versatile events space in Sri Lanka. Offers a range of flexible venues including the signature goldenlions Ballroom, the largest pillar-less ballroom in the region that can cater to seated banquets for up to 1,340 guests.The property also consists of a Lotus Ballroom catering to banquets up to 400 guests, a large outdoor space and Spices, our dedicated function rooms.
